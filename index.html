Who I Am

My name is Ariel Ongoco. I am a full stack web developer, teacher, and a musician. I love mathematics, rap music, and listing things in threes. I dislike country music, going to the dentist and finding out I have more cavities, and not listing things in threes.

Work

Part-Time Web Developer

I am currently a part-time web developer at Edward Price Company. I update a set of websites that services the International Union Pensions and its members. At work, I code in PHP (Symfony framework), JavaScript, SQL, and sometimes HTML/CSS. I work as a full stack developer, meaning I work on both back-end and front-end. I've worked on projects such as spreadsheet generation, pulling data from multiple databases and cross referencing them using RegEx, and creating buttons.

I learned how to program when near the end of 2015. While I was living in Florida, I went to starbucks for six hours a day watching YouTube videos and sifting through the internet learning about Java and Object-Oriented Programming. I continued to learn HTML/CSS and JavaScript for web development. Within a year, I had developed enough skills to get my internship. They hired me a few weeks ago for part-time on the weekends.
